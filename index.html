Let's Hack.

24-hour Hackathon hosted by students at BHHS. Do you like new tech? Are you passionate about coding? Or do you want to hang out with friends? If you answered yes to any of those, get ready for a weekend full of learning new technologies, making great friends, and pushing your limits. Tons of food, awesome gear, and everything you need to carry out your craziest ideas will be provided!

FAQ

When is Beverly Hacks?

Beverly Hacks will be from March 19 - March 20th!

Who can attend?

All students are welcome!

Where will it be?

We are hosting Beverly Hacks at Beverly Hills High School! The address is 241 Moreno Dr. Beverly Hills, CA 90212.

What should I bring?

We recommend laptops, chargers, cellphones, and of course passion. Toiletries and a change of clothes could help as well!

How big are teams?

You can have a maximum of 4 people per team!

Are we allowed to build on past projects?

A hackathon is an experience to build something new within a set amount of time. Therefore, you cannot work on past projects. Using your own open-source or third party libraries is fine as long as the project is new.
