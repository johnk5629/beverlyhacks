We make sure you have tons of swag, fun gadgets to play with, and of course, lots of Chipotle!

FAQ

When is Beverly Hacks?

Beverly Hacks will be from March 19 - March 20th starting from 6pm Thursday and ending 6pm on Friday.

Where will it be?

We are hosting Beverly Hacks at Beverly Hills High School in the STC Building! The address is 241 Moreno Dr. Beverly Hills, CA 90212.

Who can attend?

You must be a high school student to attend BeverlyHacks. Please bring your school IDs or equivalent proof of high school enrollment, your signed BeverlyHacks Code of Conduct, and your Parent Waiver to the hackathon so that we may check you in!

What should I bring?

Please bring laptops, chargers, and your passion to learn. We recommend you bring toiletries, sleeping bags, and spare clothing as we will be hacking overnight!

How big are teams?

You can have a maximum of 4 people per team! We will be helping hackers find teammates during the hackathon.

Are we allowed to build on past projects?

A hackathon is an experience to build something new within a set amount of time. Therefore, you cannot work on past projects. You may use your own open-source or third party libraries as long as the project is new.
